Things You Should Know

Things You Should Know

The Higher Education Institutions Examination (YKS) is the most important step for entering a university in Turkey. This exam consists of three stages: the Basic Proficiency Test (TYT), the Field Proficiency Test (AYT), and the Foreign Language Test (YDT), which measure the student's speed, knowledge level, and test-solving strategies.
